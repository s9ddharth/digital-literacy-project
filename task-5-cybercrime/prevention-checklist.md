## Stay Safe Online – Checklist

###  Safety Guidelines
- Never share your OTP, UPI PIN, or passwords with anyone  
- Do not approve unknown “collect requests” on UPI apps  
- Always verify the sender before making payments  
- Avoid clicking on suspicious links or messages  
- Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts  
- Enable two-factor authentication wherever possible  
- Regularly check your bank transaction history  
- Do not install unknown apps from untrusted sources  

---

###  Important Note
- You can receive money **without entering your PIN**  
- PIN is required **only for sending money**  
- Use only official apps like **Google Pay, PhonePe, or Paytm**  

---

###  Report Cybercrime
- **National Cyber Crime Portal:** https://cybercrime.gov.in  
- **Helpline Number:** 1930  

---

###  Conclusion
Following these safety practices can help prevent online fraud and ensure secure digital transactions.
